About

I've operated in the seat. I know what breaks.

I'm Alison Sullivan, Principal of Signal & Scale Advisory — a GTM strategy, Revenue Operations, and AI adoption practice for growth-stage and scaling XaaS companies.

I partner with CEOs, CROs, and PE/VC-backed leadership teams — founder-led, PE-backed, or scaling — where I can go deep and do work that moves the needle. Strategy connected to execution, with pragmatism.

My edge is that I've been in the seat. 25+ years across AI, SaaS, product, sales, and revenue operations — operator roles, board directorships, and PE portfolio work. I know what breaks at scale because I've fixed it.

Where I focus with AI: the human edge. Tools and models are increasingly commoditized — the advantage lives in how your people adopt them, adapt with them, and make them work for your specific business.

How I work

The AI GTM Sprint — a clear path from curiosity to signal.

Five weeks. One focused motion. Measurable outcomes at every gate before you scale.

1
Discovery & Prioritization
Friction mapping, use case ranking, readiness baseline
2
Diagnosis & Scope
Root cause analysis, solution design, success metrics defined
Business context, constraints, and policy fitted to the solution — so deployment is optimized for your operation, not a generic template.
3
Deploy & Instrument
AI solution live, tracking in place, governance documented
4
Signal Capture & Coaching
Adoption data, team coaching, train-the-trainer delivery
5
Handoff & Roadmap
Outcomes report, capability transfer, next sprint scoped

Three principles every engagement is built on.

Outcomes first
Business outcomes are defined before any solution is scoped. Every gate requires measurable signal. No outcomes, no next step.
Capability transfer
Train-the-trainer is structural — not optional. Your team builds real capability so the work compounds after the engagement ends.
Change by design
Change management and adoption infrastructure are built into every deliverable. Adoption is engineered, not hoped for.
